## Priority Issues

### [P2] Progress Fill Layout Transition
- **Why it matters**: Animating `width` on `.omni-progress-fill` forces the browser to recalculate layouts on every frame, which can cause jank on lower-spec machines.
- **Fix**: Replace `width` transitions with `transform: scaleX(progress)` and `transform-origin: left` to animate via GPU composites.
- **Suggested command**: `/impeccable optimize`

### [P3] Minor Radius Deviation
- **Why it matters**: `borderRadius: 1px` in the settings tab list header deviates from the standard 4px/10px/14px rounded tokens scale.
- **Fix**: Standardize to `--radius-chip` (4px) or flat corners.
- **Suggested command**: `/impeccable layout`
