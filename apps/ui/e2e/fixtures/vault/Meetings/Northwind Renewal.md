# Northwind Renewal

## Decision
We agreed to renew Northwind on a **24-month term** at a 12% uplift, contingent on
Northwind moving to the annual billing cycle. Dana confirmed her team can absorb the
uplift if we include the premium support tier at no extra cost for the first year.

## Commitments
- Send the revised order form by Friday 20 June.
- Include premium support tier, year one waived.
- Marcus to prepare the migration plan from monthly to annual billing.

## Risks
Northwind's procurement freeze lifts on 1 July, so the signed order form must land
before their quarter closes or the renewal slips a full quarter.

## Next step
Follow-up call scheduled for 25 June to walk through the migration plan.
